## Plans

Havoc offers three plans to match your needs:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Trial" icon="seedling">
    **€0 for 7 days**
    - 1 Agent
    - All channels
    - 1,000 messages
    - Full feature access
  </Card>
  <Card title="Professional" icon="building">
    **€75/agent/month**
    - Pay per agent (no limit)
    - All 13+ channels
    - 10,000 messages per agent
    - API access & analytics
    - Priority support
  </Card>
  <Card title="Enterprise" icon="city">
    **Custom pricing**
    - Unlimited agents
    - Unlimited messages
    - SSO / SAML
    - Dedicated CSM
    - On-premise option
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What Counts as Usage?

| Resource | Trial | Professional | Enterprise |
|----------|-------|---------------|------------|
| Agents | 1 | Pay per agent | Unlimited |
| Messages per agent | 1,000 | 10,000 | Unlimited |
| Channels | All | All 13+ | All |
| Team members | 1 | 5 | Custom |
| API key access | Yes | Yes | Yes |

<Note>
  AI model usage (tokens) is billed directly by your provider (Anthropic, OpenAI, etc.) through your own API keys. Havoc does not add markup to model costs.
</Note>

## Manage Subscription

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing">
    Go to **Billing** in the sidebar (or **Settings** for subscription details).
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Usage">
    See your current usage against plan limits — agents, channels, and team members.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upgrade/Downgrade">
    Click **Change Plan** to upgrade or downgrade. Changes take effect immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Billing FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I try Havoc for free?">
    Yes. Sign up for a 7-day free trial with 1 agent and full feature access. No credit card required.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my plan limits?">
    You'll receive a notification to upgrade. Existing agents continue running, but you cannot create new ones until you upgrade or remove agents.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do you charge for AI model usage?">
    No. You bring your own API keys for AI providers. Model costs are billed directly by the provider.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I cancel anytime?">
    Yes. Cancel from the Billing page. Your agents will remain active until the end of the current billing period.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
